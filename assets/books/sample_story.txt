Milo and the Moon Kite

Milo found a small silver kite under the porch. It was quiet and cool, like moonlight folded into paper.

When the evening breeze arrived, Milo whispered, "Fly gently." The kite lifted slowly above the garden.

A sleepy owl blinked from the oak tree. "That kite remembers the sky," said the owl.

Milo smiled and read the stars one by one. Every word felt bright, calm, and brave.

When the moon rose high, Milo brought the kite home. Tomorrow, there would be another page of sky to read.
